K Camp Net Worth and Estate 2022

Kristopher Campbell is a famous rapper more known under his stage name K Camp. He’s from Atlanta, Georgia and managed to acquire quite a fortune thanks to his music. As of 2023, K Camp’s net worth is estimated to be about $4.5 million.

K Camp was born April 27, 1990 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin but grew up in Atlanta, Georgia. He was musically active already at high school where he was a part of group HBC. In 2011, K Camp released his first solo mixtape called Become a Fan. Some of the later mixtapes were Workaholics, Slum Lords, and Show Money.
