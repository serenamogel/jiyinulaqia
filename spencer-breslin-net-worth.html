Spencer Breslin Wiki Biography

Spencer Breslin was born on the 18th May 1992, in New York City USA, and is an award-winning actor and musician, perhaps still best known to the world as Rusty Duritz from the film “The Kid” (2000), for which he won a Young Artist Award in the category Best Performance in a Feature Film – Young Actor Age Ten or Under, and as Josh in the film “The Happening” (2008), among other differing appearances. Spencer’s career started in the mid- ‘90s, becoming a child star.

Have you ever wondered how rich Spencer Breslin is, as of early 2017? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that Breslin’s net worth is as high as $5 million, an amount earned through his successful career in the entertainment industry.

Spencer is the middle child of Kim and Michael Breslin. His father is a telecommunications expert and consultant, while his mother is a personal manager, He and his siblings were home-schooled, and according to Spencer, his parents always supporting young Spencer in his interests. When he was three years old, a talent scout spotted him on a playground, and soon Spencer’s career was off to a remarkable start, featuring in over 50 commercials, including for McDonald’s and Life cereal, among many other. Then in 1997 he made his acting debut in the TV series “Soul Man” as Fred Weber, and two years later was cast as Donny Beals in the TV Mini-series “Storm of the Century”. In 2000 he made his big screen debut in the comedy “The Kid”, next to Bruce Willis and Emily Mortimer., which really launched him into the film industry, as new roles followed in the blink of an eye. The same year he featured in the romantic comedy “Meet the Parents”, and then in 2002 played Curtis in the film “The Santa Clause”. In 2003 he was cast as Conrad in the comedy “The Cat in the Hat” next to Mike Myers, which further increased his wealth, and then in 2004 he was selected for the role of Miles Barnett in the comedy TV series “Center of the Universe” (2004-2005). Also in 2004 he featured with his sister Abigail in the film “Raising Helen”, which grossed $122 million at box office, helping increasing his net worth to a large degree. Two years later he repeated his role of Curtis in the sequel “The Santa Clause 3: The Escape Clause”, while in 2008 he played the title role in the comedy “Harold”, and Josh in the sci-fi thriller “The Happening”, with Mark Wahlberg and Zooey Deschanel, which also added to his wealth. Recently, Spencer again featured together with his sister, this time in the thriller drama “Perfect Sisters” (2014), while in 2015 he had the lead role in the horror “Some Kind of Hate”.

Apart from acting, Spencer is also an accomplished musician; he released the solo album “Labor Day” in 2009, and as of 2016 has been the frontman of the band Broken Machine. This has also benefited his net worth.
