Miranda Otto Net Worth: How Much Is Miranda Otto Worth?: Born on December 16, 1967, Miranda Otto is an Australian actress.

She developed a passion for acting at a younger age and in 1986 at age 18, she appeared in a variety of independent and major studio films in Australia

Otto graduated from the National Institute of Dramatic Art in Sydney and prior to graduation, she appeared in minor film roles including Initiation and The 13th Floor.

Otto has been consistent throughout her career and has eventually risen to become one of the most sought-after Australian actresses.

After a decade of several roles in Australian films, Otto gained Hollywood’s attention during the 1990s for her supporting roles in the films; The Thin Red Line and What Lies Beneath.

Her first major Hollywood role was the suspense thriller What Lies Beneath in 2000 where she played Mary Feur, a mysterious next-door neighbor.

Otto became a household name in the early 2000s for playing Éowyn in Peter Jackson’s The Lord of the Rings film series, a role she will reprise in the 2024 prequel film The Lord of the Rings: The War of the Rohirrim.

Her other film credit includes; The Girl Who Came Late, The Last Days of Chez Nous, The Nostradamus Kid, Sex Is a Four Letter Word, Love Serenade, The Well, and Doing Time for Patsy Cline, among others.

In May 2020, Otto joined Josh Gad’s YouTube series Reunited Apart, which reunites the cast of popular films through video conferencing.

Otto will reprise her role as Eowyn in The Lord of the Rings film series in the upcoming The Lord of the Rings: The War of the Rohirrim (2024) to which she would serve as the narrator.

Miranda Otto Net Worth: How Much Is Miranda Otto Worth?

As of July 2023, Miranda Otto has an estimated net worth of about $4 million. The Australian actress has earned such an amount from her successful career in film, television, and theatre.
