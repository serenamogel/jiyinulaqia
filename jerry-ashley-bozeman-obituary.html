While we mourn his loss, let us also celebrate the life he lived and the legacy he leaves behind.

Family Members Who Passed Away Before

Jerry Ashley Bozeman was preceded in death by several beloved family members who held a special place in his heart. Among them were his mother, Deborah Bozeman, his grandfather affectionately known as Papaw Parker (MaMaw Parker), his cousin Kecia, and his son Jacob Blaine Bozeman. While they may no longer be with us, their memories will forever be cherished and their spirits will continue to live on in the hearts of those who loved them.

Family Members Carrying on His Legacy

Jerry’s legacy lives on through the love and support of his surviving family members. His father, Jerry Bozeman, his child Rain Bigner, and his sons-in-law Thomas Bigner, James Brandon Cader, Aaron Joy, and Josh Berry are all dedicated to preserving his memory and upholding the values he held dear. Additionally, his sister Wendi Pharis and his nephews Luke Lincoln, Caleb Lincoln, and Adam Lincoln will continue to honor Jerry’s legacy through their own lives.

Graveside Service Information

The service will take place at the Taylor Chapel located within the serene grounds of Summerville Cemetery. We invite all friends, family, and loved ones to join us in paying our final respects to a remarkable individual who touched the lives of many.
