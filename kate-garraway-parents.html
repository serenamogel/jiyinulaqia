Kate Garraway Wikipedia & Biography

Kate Garraway Wikipedia: - Kate Garraway was born Kathryn Mary Draper Garraway on 4 May 1967 in Abingdon-on-Thames, Berkshire, England. She is an English broadcaster and journalist. She went to Fitzharrys School and Dunmore Primary School in Abingdon. She later earned a history and English degree from Bath College of Higher Education. Garraway's broadcasting career started with BBC Radio Oxford, and by 1994, she had advanced to the position of trainee journalist for Independent Television News.

On Smooth Radio, Garraway hosts Mid Mornings with Kate Garraway. On ITV, she co-anchors Good Morning Britain on Fridays and reads the news on Thursdays. Garraway represents the Make-A-Wish Foundation as a charitable ambassador. In the 2022 New Year Honours, Garraway was named a Member of the Order of the British Empire (MBE) for his contributions to charity, journalism, and radio. On March 9, 2017, she published The Joy of Big Knickers, her debut novel.

Kate Garraway Career

Lifestyle & Career: Garraway began working as a production journalist, reporter, and news presenter on ITV Central's South edition of ITV News Central in 1989. She started working as a co-presenter on ITV Meridian's South East version of ITV News in 1996. Garraway joined Sunrise on Sky News in 1998 alongside Martin Stanford. Garraway began working for GMTV in September 2000, co-hosting GMTV Today every Friday with Andrew Castle. In 2004, she hosted one episode of Too Many Cooks. She asked questions on The People's Quiz in 2007. Garraway hosted The Biggest Loser on ITV in 2009. A while later, Davina McCall took her position.

In September 2010, she was appointed entertainment editor of Daybreak on ITV Breakfast, which replaced GMTV. On April 25, 2014, she hosted her final episodes of Daybreak and Lorraine. Garraway occasionally hosted The National Lottery Draws on BBC One from March 2014 until November 2016. She made a guest appearance on Hollyoaks in September 2017. Garraway finished fourth in the nineteenth season of I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here in November 2019. In the fifth season of Strictly Come Dancing, Garraway participated with dancer Anton du Beke.
