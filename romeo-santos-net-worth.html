Romeo Santos | Career

Romeo, his cousin Henry Santos, and his pals Lenny Santos and Max Santos founded the band Aventura in 1994.

He was the band’s lead vocalist, primary songwriter, and co-producer. Three of the group members were from The Bronx, while one was born in the Dominican Republic.

Intending to take bachata music out of its traditional base and fuse it with hip hop and R&B, the band released its debut album in 1999.

The song “Obsesión” was a smash hit, reaching the top of the foreign charts in France, Germany, and Italy.

In a decade, Aventura released five studio albums, many of which produced top 10 songs, including the internationally renowned “Cuando Volverás,” “Un Beso,” and “Dile Al Amor.”

Unfortunately, the band disbanded in 2011.
