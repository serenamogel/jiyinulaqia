Biography

Johana Amaya was born in Cali, Colombia on August 4, 1981. Journalist and television presenter who is known for being the host of the RCN News Broadcast. She is also recognized for working as a presenter on the National Police program Seguridad al Dia on Channel One in 2004. She interviewed actor Ivan Lopez on Noticias RCN and shared a photo with him to her Instagram in June of 2018. She studied Social Communication and Journalism at the Universidad Autonoma de Occidente. Her career first began as a presenter and reporter for the 90 Minute newscast Telepacifico in 2002. After several other reporting jobs, she began working with RCN Television in 2006.
