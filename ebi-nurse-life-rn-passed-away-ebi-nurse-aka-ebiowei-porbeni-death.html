Ebi Nurse Life RN Passed Away Ebi Nurse Aka Ebiowei Porbeni Death

Ebi from the Nurse Life RN has sadly passed away. The death news of Ebiowei Porbeni followed his Cancer struggles.

Ebiowei Porbeni or Ebi was an American ICU Nurse and entrepreneur. Porbeni is the owner of a media company Nurselifern, a company specializing in Nurse recruitment and social media marketing. 

Ebi managed to create a huge impact on the Nurselifern community. Many fans and friends have taken to  Twitter to mourn the death of Ebiowei Porbeni and have shared their condolences.

Ebiowei Porbeni Death Details Explained

The creator of Nurse Life RN or nurselifern, Ebiowei Porbeni died on July 22, 2021.

Porbeni battled cancer for a very long time. In October 2020, Ebiowei Porbeni disclosed that he was diagnosed with Leukaemia. Ebi took his last breath surrounded by family, friends, and loved ones on Tuesday afternoon.

Ebi’s untimely death has shocked many of his friends and fans. Ebiowei Porbeni is survived by his parents and three brothers.
