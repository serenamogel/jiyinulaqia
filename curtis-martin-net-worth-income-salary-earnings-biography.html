Curtis Martin Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Curtis James Martin, Jr. (born May 1, 1973) is a retired American football running back who played for the New England Patriots and the New York Jets in the National Football League (NFL) during the course of a ten-year career. Martin started his professional football career with the New England Patriots, who drafted him in the third round of the 1995 National Football League Draft. 

In 1998, he signed with the New York Jets as a free agent, and he played there until 2005, when he suffered a career-ending knee injury that ended his playing career. He retired with the fourth-highest amount of running yards in the history of the National Football League. In 2012, he was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame as a defensive back.
