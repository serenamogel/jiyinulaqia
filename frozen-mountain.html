Frozen Mountain (Japanese: 凍結の山 Frozen Mountain) is a dungeon in Pokémon Super Mystery Dungeon that is located on the Mist Continent. It is unlocked after completing Midnight Sun Gorge as part of Sealeo's Connection Orb mission.
