The late 1990s marked a significant turning point in Cardellini’s career when she was cast as Lindsay Weir in the cult television series “Freaks and Geeks.” Although the show had a short run, it gained a devoted following and is often credited with launching the careers of several of its young stars, including Cardellini.

Following “Freaks and Geeks,” Cardellini continued to build her portfolio with a variety of roles.
