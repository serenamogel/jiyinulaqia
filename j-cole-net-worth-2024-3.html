Early Life and Career Beginnings

J. Cole was born on a military base in Germany and raised in Fayetteville, North Carolina. He began rapping at a young age and pursued a music career earnestly after graduating from St. John’s University in New York City. His early mixtapes garnered significant attention, leading to a record deal with Roc Nation, founded by Jay-Z.

J. Cole’s discography includes several critically acclaimed albums, such as “2014 Forest Hills Drive,” “Born Sinner,” and “KOD.” His albums have consistently sold well, often achieving platinum status, which has significantly contributed to his net worth.
