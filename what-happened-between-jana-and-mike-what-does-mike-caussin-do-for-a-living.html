What happened between Jana and Mike? What does Mike Caussin do for a living?

Multiple sources announced that the actress and country musician Jana Kramer and former NFL star Mike Caussin were splitting up after publicizing their ups and downs for years.

What happened between Jana and Mike?

Kramer began dating Washington Redskins tight end Mike Caussin, whom she met on Twitter, in August 2014. Kramer filed for divorce from Caussin on April 20, 2021, claiming “inappropriate marital conduct, irreconcilable disagreements, and infidelity.” A temporary restraining order was issued by a Williamson County, Tennessee court two days after the couple split up.

Kramer and Caussin struck a custody and child support arrangement on May 20, 2021, according to reports. Kramer was given primary custody of Jolie and Jace, according to the records. Caussin will have the children for the remaining 125 days of the year, while she will have them for 240 days.

Kramer must also pay Caussin $3,200 in child support each month. On July 22, 2021, the divorce was completed.

Caussin was in therapy for sex addiction after several affairs, it was revealed less than two years into their marriage.

What does Mike Caussin do for a living?

Caussin is a former American football tight end who spent time with the Buffalo Bills, Jacksonville Jaguars, and Washington Redskins, among other teams.
