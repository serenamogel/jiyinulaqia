Biography

Victor Ponta was born in Romania on September 20, 1972. Social Democratic Party politician who took office as Prime Minister of Romania in 2012. He entered the 2014 Romanian presidential election, but lost to National Liberal Party politician Klaus Iohannis. He served as Prime Minister under the presidency of Traian Basescu. Before beginning his political career, he earned a law degree from the University of Bucharest and became a prosecutor for Romania’s High Court of Cassation and Justice.
