    Slow Cooker Spiced Cranberry Apple Citrus Cider

    This Slow Cooker Spiced Cranberry Citrus Apple Cider is the beverage of choice this season! Fill your home with it's delightful fragrance and your belly with it's spicy warmth! Perfect for parties and the holidays!

    Course Beverage Cuisine American Keyword apple citrus cider, cider, citrus cider, slow cooker spiced cranberry apple citrus cider Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 2 hours hours Total Time 2 hours hours 5 minutes minutes Servings 16 servings Calories 133kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 64 oz cranberry juice
    • 64 oz apple cider or juice
    • 33.8 oz Green Tea Citrus Flavored Iced Tea 2 16.9 oz bottles
    • 1 orange sliced
    • 1 lemon sliced
    • 1 lime sliced
    • 5 cinnamon sticks
    • 5 whole cloves up to 10

    Instructions

    • Stick the cloves into the rind of the orange slices. This way nobody gets a surprise in their mug ðŸ™‚

    • Combine the cranberry juice, apple cider, and Lipton Green Tea Citrus Flavored Iced Tea in a large slow cooker.

    • Add as many slices of citrus fruit as you like, including the orange slice with the cloves in it.

    • Set your slow cooker to low for 6 to 8 hours or high for 2 to 4 hours. Once desired temperature is reached, turn your slow cooker to warm.

    • Serve with a fruit garnish or cinnamon stick.
