Classic Multiplayer Character Pack Announced For Max Payne 3

Recommended Videos

A special downloadable pack for Max Payne 3‘s multiplayer mode, featuring characters from earlier games in the franchise, has been announced. Titled the Classic Multiplayer Character Pack, it will contain playable versions of Vladimir “Vlad” Lem, Vincent “Vinnie” Gognitti, Jack Lupino, Alfred Woden, Mona Sax, Valerie Winterson, Nicole Horne, and the NYPD version of the series’ title character from Max Payne 2.

The pack will initially be available for free, exclusively to those who purchase the game’s Special Edition, though Rockstar has also confirmed that it will be released to the general public at some point. However, a price was not given.
