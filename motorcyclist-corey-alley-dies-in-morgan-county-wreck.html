Tragic Loss: Motorcyclist Corey Alley Dies in Morgan County Crash

On Wednesday night, tragedy struck as Corey Alley,

a 35-year-old resident of Lake Ozark, lost his life in a motorcycle accident in Morgan County. The incident occurred around 9:30 p.m. on Route Y, approximately 1.2 miles north of Brendel Boulevard.

Corey was riding his 2016 Indian Scout motorcycle in the southbound lane when, regrettably, he veered off the right side of the road, colliding with a tree and being forcefully ejected from the motorcycle.

The heartbreaking accident claimed Corey Alley’s life, and he was pronounced deceased at the scene by the deputy medical examiner on September 28 at 3:40 p.m. The next of kin has been duly informed.

Corey Alley’s body has been entrusted to the care of the Boone County Medical Examiner’s Office.
