Joel Robuchon Net Worth

Joel Robuchon was a French chef and restaurateur born on April 7, 1945 in Poitiers, France. He was voted Chef of the Century in 1989 and was renowned for his innovative and creative approach to French cuisine. He was the first chef to receive the maximum three Michelin stars for each of his restaurants, and he was also the first chef to create a global restaurant empire.
