Christina Applegate Biography

Christina Applegate was born on November 25, 1971, in Los Angeles, California, United States. Her parents are Robert Applegate and Nancy Priddy. Nancy was an actress, and Robert was a record producer. After her divorce, her mother began dating musician Stephen Stills and eventually married him.

Career

Christina Applegate rose to prominence as Kelly Bundy on the Fox sitcom Married……….. with Children (1987–1997). In 1972, she made her television debut alongside her mother on Days of Our Lives. Later, at 3 and 5 months, she played the lead in a commercial for Playtex baby bottles. She made her debut in the horror film Jaws Of Satan in 1981, followed by Beatlemania in 1981.

Christina Applegate was a founding member of The Pussycat Dolls, who made their debut in 1995 at Johnny Depp’s Viper Room on the Sunset Strip. The Pussycat Doll, of which she was a founding member, made its debut. She was the group’s emcee when they relocated to The Roxy Theatre in 2002. Christina auditioned for the role of Rose in the film Titanic in 1996 but lost to Kate Winslet. Following that, in 1998, she began playing the role of Jesse in the NBC sitcom Jesse. That series premiered in 1998 and received acclaim. She won the People’s Choice Award for Favorite Female Performer in a New TV Series and the TV Guide Award for Favorite Star in a New Series. Aside from these, she was nominated for Best Actress in a Comedy at the Golden Globes.

She has also appeared on stage in productions such as The Axeman’s Jazz and Nobody Leaves Empty Handed, The Runthrough, and John Cassavetes’ The Third Day (co-starring Gena Rowlands).

In July 2018, she co-starred with Linda Cardellini in the Netflix dark comedy series Dead to Me. She also co-produced the series with Will Ferrell, Adam McKay, and Jessica Elbaum, which debuted to critical acclaim.

Moreover, Christina Applegate also starred in the NBC sitcom Jesse (1998–2000), for which she received a Golden Globe nomination.

Christina Applegate is currently single and unmarried. On the other hand, Christina Applegate married actor Johnathon Schaech in Palm Springs, California, on October 20, 2001. In December 2005, she divorced him. In 2013, she married Martyn LeNoble.
