Julia Garner Looks Like A Different Person In Her New Netflix Role

Julia Garner was spotted filming scenes for Inventing Anna in New York City in November 2020, offering fans a sneak peak at her character Anna Delvey's costume choices and the star's unrecognizable new hairstyle. In photos obtained by Looper, the Emmy award-winning actor traded in her short curly hair for a long and straight hairstyle. She is also in an extravagant outfit, complete with a red designer handbag and black leather gloves. 

But wait, there's more! The Daily Mail also published photos of Garner with bright red hair and wearing a purple pantsuit. Both these hair 'dos are far from what fans of the Ozark star are used to; Garner's short, curly blonde hairstyle has become her calling card. 

No need to worry, for Garner's golden curls will likely be back when she reprises her role as the sassy and smart Ruth Langmore in the final season of Ozark, which was renewed back in June 2020.
