Do psychopaths have no emotions?

published on January 22, 2024
Psychopaths comprise about 1% of the general population and can be productive members of society. Their lack of emotions, such as anxiety and fear, helps them to stay calm in frightening situations. Experiments have shown that they have a reduced startle response. Can psychopaths be emotional? Summary: Contrary to popular belief, those suffering from psychopathy are able to experience emotions, but they do have a blunted emotional response if their attention is directed toward something else.
