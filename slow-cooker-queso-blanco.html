    Slow Cooker Queso Blanco

    Spicy, cheesy, and totally delicious, this easy Slow Cooker Queso Blanco is what you want to be serving at your next party!

    Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword queso blanco, queso blanco dip Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 2 hours hours Total Time 2 hours hours 5 minutes minutes Servings 8 servings Calories 217kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 8 ounces cream cheese cut into cubes
    • 8 ounces pepper jack cheese grated
    • 4 ounces diced green chiles do not drain
    • 2 tablespoons diced pimientos drained
    • ¼ cup evaporated milk plus more as needed
    • pickled jalapeno slices optional

    Instructions

    • Add all of the ingredients to a small 2 qt slow cooker.

    • Stir gently.

    • Heat on low for 2 - 3 hours, stirring every 30 minutes.

    • Add additional milk as necessary for your desired consistency.
